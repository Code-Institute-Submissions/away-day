AwayDay
AwayDay exists to give travelling supporters a quick guide to their next destination.

Select your opponent from the drop-down menu at the the top of the page, then click each button to customise the map.

Clicking the 'Get to the ground' button will generation directions from your current location.

Clicking any of the 'Best...' buttons will display the top-rated businesses in each category.

Click the 'SHARE' button to send the map to the friends you are travelling with!
